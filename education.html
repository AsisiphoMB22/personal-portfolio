Education

I am working towards an Advanced diploma qualification in Application Development and here are some of the projects I've worked on throughout the years.

An online book ordering system

This was meant to help high schools from rural areas so that they can order required textbooks on time for their matric students.

An online BnB Booking system

Our instructions were to create a website that will help a certain BnB with handling bookings, bookkeeping and also help avoid double bookings.

A portable electricity backup rental system

Our aim was to make a website to help East London citizens with renting portable electric backup devices to help during loadshedding.

Working on these with my classmates and peers has been a roller coaster but of course educational.
